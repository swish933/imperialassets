Portfolio Management
This product allows clients to invest in a mix of asset classes with a focus on capital preservation and multiple revenue streams. The asset classes include:
Treasury Bills (T-Bills)
Offers good yields and aims at improving price stability in Nigeria. Promotes portfolio stability as commonly considered a sovereign risk. Maturities are 91 days, 182 days, and 364 days.
Commercial Papers (CP)
Short-term instruments, similar to T-Bills maturities but offers higher yields. CPs are issued by corporate entities.
Equities
Our affiliate company has experienced brokers that manage healthy portfolios. We help clients trade at the NGX.
Naira Bonds
Offers a capital appreciation opportunity based on number of days held. This is a government backed security.
Mutual Funds
Mutual funds are designed for investors seeking to optimize their returns at minimal risks.
Euro Bonds
Considering the foreign exchange volatility, this product aids to hedge against it. Its features are similar to Naira bonds.
Arbitrage & Financial Intermediation Services
