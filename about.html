A Stockbroking and Financial Advisory Company
Imperial Asset Managers Limited (“Imperial”, “the Company”) was incorporated in Nigeria on October 19, 2006 as a private limited liability company. Imperial is duly licensed by the Nigerian Stock Exchange (NSE) and registered by the Securities & Exchange Commission. The Company commenced business operations on 3rd September 2007.

MISSION
To share in the joy of enhancing our customer’s wealth, and experience the excitement of competing and winning in the market place.


VISION
To seek superiority in all we undertake to do and to be among the top 10 stock broking firms in Nigeria.




OUR VALUES

Friendliness
Friendliness is the bedrock on which we build our relationships.
Innovation
We are dedicated to evolving as a people and providing better and improved solutions that add value to our clients.
Responsiveness
We are responsive to our client's needs and inquiries, providing solutions and resolutions quickly and efficiently.
